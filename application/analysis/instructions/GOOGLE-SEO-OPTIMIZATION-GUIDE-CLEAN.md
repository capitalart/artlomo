# Google SEO Optimization Guide for Etsy Listings

Version: 2.0 Clean
Purpose: Practical Google-aware SEO guide for Robin Custance / BOAND Art Etsy digital art listings.

## Core Principle
Write for buyers first, then help search engines understand the page. Google’s guidance prioritises helpful, reliable, people-first content and clear wording that matches what people search for.

## What Google Needs to Understand
For each listing, make these clear:
- What the artwork is
- What subject or location it shows
- What style or mood it has
- That it is a digital download
- That it is printable wall art
- Who created it
- What kind of room or buyer it suits

## Title Strategy
Lead with the strongest search phrase. Use natural language.

Good pattern:

```text
Australian Landscape Wall Art - Flinders Ranges Digital Download - Large Printable Artwork - Robin Custance
```

Title components:
1. Main search phrase
2. Subject or location
3. Format
4. Size/value cue
5. Artist/brand

Avoid repetitive keyword stuffing, vague poetic-only titles, irrelevant keywords, and misleading physical-product terms.

## First Paragraph Strategy
Google and buyers both benefit from a clear first paragraph.

Include subject, location or setting, style/mood, digital download, and artist name.

Example:

```text
Bring the warm colour and rugged beauty of the Flinders Ranges into your home with this large-format digital download wall art by South Australian artist Robin Custance.
```

## Helpful Content Signals
A strong listing answers buyer questions:
- What do I receive?
- How do I print it?
- What size can it print?
- What room will it suit?
- Is anything shipped?
- Can I use it commercially?
- Who made it?

## Keyword Strategy
Use natural keyword clusters.

Primary keyword examples:
- Australian landscape wall art
- Aboriginal Australian digital art
- printable wall art
- digital download artwork
- South Australian art
- native animal wall art
- Flinders Ranges art
- coastal wall decor
- large wall art download

Secondary keyword examples:
- living room wall art
- bedroom wall decor
- office wall print
- earthy Australian decor
- nature inspired art
- warm neutral artwork
- statement wall art

## Description Structure for SEO

```text
Opening hook

What the artwork shows

What you receive

Printing guidance

Where it works

Artist note

Digital download and no-shipping note

Licence note
```

## Image SEO
For owned websites, use descriptive image filenames and alt text. On Etsy, use clear listing images and mockups that match the product. Avoid misleading images that imply a framed physical item is included.

Example image filename for owned site:

```text
flinders-ranges-australian-landscape-wall-art-robin-custance.jpg
```

Example alt text:

```text
Digital artwork of the Flinders Ranges in warm evening light by Robin Custance.
```

## Google-Safe AI Content Approach
AI-assisted content should still be useful, accurate, original, and human-reviewed. Do not mass-publish thin, repetitive, or misleading listings. Each listing should contain artwork-specific details.

## Internal Consistency
Keep wording consistent across title, first paragraph, tags/keywords, mockup text, artwork details, and file naming where possible.

## SEO Checklist
- [ ] Title starts with a buyer-search phrase.
- [ ] First paragraph clearly describes the artwork.
- [ ] Digital download is stated.
- [ ] No physical shipping is stated.
- [ ] Description answers buyer questions.
- [ ] Keywords are natural and relevant.
- [ ] No unrelated keyword stuffing.
- [ ] Artist name is included where appropriate.
- [ ] Location/subject details are accurate.
- [ ] Listing is unique enough to avoid thin duplicate content.
