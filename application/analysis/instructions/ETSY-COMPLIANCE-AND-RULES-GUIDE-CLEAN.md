# Etsy Listing Compliance and Rules Guide

Version: 2.0 Clean
Purpose: Practical compliance guide for BOAND Art / Robin Custance Etsy digital art listings.

## Core Rule
Every listing must be honest, clear, and consistent with what the buyer receives.

For Robin Custance digital art listings, the buyer receives a digital file. Therefore every listing must clearly say:
- Digital download
- No physical item is shipped
- Printing/framing is the buyer’s responsibility unless otherwise stated

## What Can Be Sold
Etsy’s marketplace is for items that fit its creativity standards, including items made, designed, handpicked, or sourced by a seller. Digital artwork designed and prepared by the seller can fit this model when the listing is clear and truthful.

## Digital Download Requirements
Each listing should include:
- File type
- Pixel dimensions if known
- Recommended print size if known
- Delivery method
- No physical shipping statement
- Personal-use licence note unless a commercial licence is included
- Basic print guidance

Recommended wording:

```text
This is a digital download. No physical item will be shipped.
```

## Avoid Misleading Physical Product Language
Do not use wording that suggests the buyer receives a framed print, canvas, poster, mounted artwork, paper print, shipped item, or handmade physical object unless the listing actually includes that physical product.

## AI-Assisted Art Disclosure
Do not claim the artwork is traditionally hand-painted if it is AI-assisted digital art. Use honest wording such as digital artwork, AI-assisted digital artwork, artist-directed digital artwork, or digitally refined artwork.

## Cultural and Heritage Accuracy
Allowed when factual:
- Robin Custance is a South Australian digital artist.
- Robin Custance has Boandik / Bunganditj family connection.
- The Boandik / Bunganditj are connected with the Naracoorte / Mount Gambier region.
- “People of the Reeds” may be used when already part of the artist’s approved identity language.

Avoid invented Dreaming stories, sacred/ceremonial claims, claims of official cultural authority, location-specific cultural claims not supplied by the artist, or language that turns heritage into a sales gimmick.

## Intellectual Property Safety
Do not use copyrighted characters, brand names as subjects, celebrity likenesses without rights, another artist’s name in a way that implies collaboration/authorship/endorsement, or protected Indigenous designs/stories without permission.

## Listing Title
A title should be clear, searchable, and truthful.

Good pattern:

```text
[Subject or Location] Wall Art - [Style/Mood] Digital Download - Large Printable Artwork - Robin Custance
```

Avoid keyword stuffing, false physical-product terms, unsupported “museum certified” or “investment piece” claims, and unrelated trending keywords.

## Description
A good compliant description includes:
1. What the artwork shows
2. What the buyer receives
3. Digital download disclosure
4. No physical shipping disclosure
5. Print guidance
6. Artist note
7. Usage suggestions
8. Colour/print variation note
9. Licence note

## Images and Mockups
Mockups must not mislead the buyer into thinking a physical framed print is included. Include clear text in the listing that the purchase is a digital download only.

## Personal Use Licence
Default wording:

```text
This purchase is for personal use only. You may print it for your own home, office, or as a personal gift. Commercial use, resale, redistribution, or uploading the file to print-on-demand marketplaces is not included unless a separate commercial licence is provided.
```

## Compliance Checklist
- [ ] Title is truthful.
- [ ] Description says digital download.
- [ ] Description says no physical item is shipped.
- [ ] File details are accurate.
- [ ] Print guidance is clear.
- [ ] No unsupported cultural claims.
- [ ] No misleading physical-product wording.
- [ ] No unauthorised IP references.
- [ ] Licence terms are included.
- [ ] Mockups do not override the digital-only disclosure.
